# English Pancakes
### Crepe-like pancakes

#### Ingredients
- 3/4 cup flour
- 1/8 tsp salt
- 2 Tbsp sugar
- 1/4 tsp nutmeg
- 3 eggs
- 1/2 cup heavy cream
- 3 Tbsp melted butter
- 4 Tbsp sherry

#### Preparation
1. Sift dry ingredients
2. Beat in eggs, cream, butter, and sherry
3. Beat well
4. Cook like crepes in 6in skillet (medium heat)
